Hello, This is the left Diary.

Welcome to The Left Diary where you can find summaries and key notes of left leaning books. It was created to share our love of books and their authors and to give you insights about, in a concise manner, what can be learnt from each the books.

The importance of reading lot of books and being well versed in theory is well known among the left leaning community. Hence, to make an index, keep track, and have quick notes about the books, an article is dedicated to each of the books read/studied by us.

It must be noted that the articles in this blog are in no way a replacement of the actual book reading and we urge you to read the original book.
